Wave Function Physics

In the following sections, you will find the explanation about the topics that will help to understand the wave function. The objectives of this section are:

  • Identify the classic wave physics.
  • Compare and relate the classic physics with quantum mechanics.
  • Analyze the use of complex numbers in quantum mechanics.

After the study of this concepts, you will be able yo recognize how these elements are reflected in the wave function and move forward to the next section.
